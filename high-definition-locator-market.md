<p><p>The High-definition Locator market is categorized into two main types: GNSS (Global Navigation Satellite System) and INS (Inertial Navigation System). GNSS applications leverage satellite signals for precise positioning and navigation, making it essential for outdoor environments. Conversely, INS utilizes onboard sensors to track movement by measuring acceleration and rotation, providing accurate positioning in environments where satellite signals may be weak or unavailable. Both systems enhance location accuracy and reliability across various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and robotics.</p></p>
