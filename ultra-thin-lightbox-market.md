<p><ul><li>LED</li><li>EEFL</li><li>T4 Fluorescent Bulb</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra Thin Lightbox market encompasses three primary types: LED, EEFL (External Electrode Fluorescent Lamp), and T4 fluorescent bulbs. LED lightboxes are energy-efficient, offer long lifespans, and provide vibrant colors, making them popular for modern displays. EEFL lightboxes utilize external electrodes for greater efficiency and brightness compared to traditional fluorescent options. T4 fluorescent bulbs are compact and provide even illumination but are less energy-efficient. Each type caters to different lighting needs and applications in advertising, interior design, and exhibition displays.</p></p>
