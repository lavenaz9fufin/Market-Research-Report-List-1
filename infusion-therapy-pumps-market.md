<p><p>Infusion therapy pumps are classified into two primary market types: Type I and Type II. Type I pumps are typically used in hospitals and healthcare facilities for critical care, delivering precise medication dosages. They often include features like continuous infusion and advanced alarm systems to ensure patient safety. Type II pumps cater to home healthcare and outpatient settings, designed for patient convenience, portability, and ease of use, focusing on infusing medications or nutrients in a less intensive care environment.</p></p>
