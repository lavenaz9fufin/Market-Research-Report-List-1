<p><strong>The Run-Out Gauge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Run-Out</li><li>Axial Run-Out</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Run-Out Gauge market is categorized into two primary types: Radial Run-Out and Axial Run-Out. Radial Run-Out measures the deviation in the rotational axis of a cylindrical component as it spins, indicating eccentricity. Axial Run-Out measures the linear displacement along the axis of rotation, assessing how much a part moves away from its intended path lengthwise. Both types are essential in quality control processes for manufacturing, ensuring precision in engineering applications, and maintaining optimal performance in machinery.</p></p>
