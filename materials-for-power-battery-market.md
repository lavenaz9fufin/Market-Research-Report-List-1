<p><p>The materials for the power battery market consist of several key components. Cathode materials, such as lithium cobalt oxide and nickel manganese cobalt, play a crucial role in energy capacity. Anode materials, primarily graphite, facilitate lithium-ion storage and release. Lithium-ion battery separators prevent short-circuits by keeping the cathode and anode apart while allowing ion flow. Electrolytes, typically liquid or solid polymer compounds, enable ionic conductivity within the battery, ensuring efficient charge and discharge cycles for optimal performance.</p></p>
