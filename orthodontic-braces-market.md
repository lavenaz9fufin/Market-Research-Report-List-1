<p><p>The orthodontic braces market comprises several types tailored to various patient needs. Traditional braces use metal brackets and wires for effective tooth alignment. Ceramic braces, while similar to traditional ones, blend with teeth for a discreet appearance. Clear aligners are removable, transparent trays that gradually shift teeth, appealing to those seeking aesthetics and comfort. Lingual braces are attached to the back of teeth, making them invisible from the front, ideal for self-conscious patients. Each type offers distinct benefits and caters to different preferences.</p></p>
