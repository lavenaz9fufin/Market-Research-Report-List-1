<p><ul><li>One-component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PU foam sealant is categorized into one-component and two-component types. One-component foam is pre-packaged and requires no mixing, expanding upon exposure to moisture, making it user-friendly for DIY projects. Two-component foam involves mixing two separate components, offering higher performance, better adhesion, and durability, making it suitable for professional applications. Both types are ideal for insulation, sealing gaps, and soundproofing, with the choice depending on the specific requirements of the project, such as application method and environmental conditions.</p></p>
