<p><p>The bio-based butadiene market consists of two primary production methods: one-step and multi-step methods. One-step methods involve direct conversion of biomass into butadiene, streamlining production for efficiency and reduced costs. In contrast, multi-step methods entail several processes, where biomass is first transformed into intermediates before being converted into butadiene. While multi-step methods offer flexibility in feedstock variety and yield optimization, one-step methods prioritize simplicity and reduced processing times, making each suitable for distinct market applications.</p></p>
