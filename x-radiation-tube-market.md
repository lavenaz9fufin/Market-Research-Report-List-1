<p><strong>The X-Radiation Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Anode</li><li>Rotating Anode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-Radiation Tube market is primarily divided into two types: stationary anode and rotating anode tubes. Stationary anode tubes utilize a fixed target for X-ray generation, typically offering high image quality for lower power applications but are limited in heat management. In contrast, rotating anode tubes feature a rotating target, allowing for better heat dissipation and higher output, making them suitable for advanced imaging techniques in medical and industrial applications. Both types are essential for various X-ray equipment.</p></p>
