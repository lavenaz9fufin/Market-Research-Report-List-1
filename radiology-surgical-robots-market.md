<p><p>The radiology surgical robots market comprises three main types: X-ray based, gamma-ray based, and proton-beam based systems. X-ray based robots utilize imaging to guide surgical procedures with high accuracy and minimal invasiveness. Gamma-ray based systems focus on targeted radiation therapy for localized tumors, enhancing treatment precision. Proton-beam based technology employs protons to deliver radiation with reduced damage to surrounding tissues, ideal for complex cases. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to various clinical needs in precision medicine.</p></p>
