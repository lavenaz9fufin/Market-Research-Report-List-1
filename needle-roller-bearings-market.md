<p><p>The needle roller bearings market is divided into single-row and double-row bearings. Single-row bearings feature a single row of needle rollers, offering minimal space requirements and high load-carrying capacity, making them ideal for compact applications. Double-row bearings consist of two rows of needles, providing enhanced load capacity and stability, suited for heavier and more demanding applications. Both types are crucial in reducing friction in machinery and improving efficiency in various industries, including automotive and manufacturing.</p></p>
